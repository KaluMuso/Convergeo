## Two simple cases

- **Before funds are released:** your refund comes **directly from escrow** — fast and safe.
- **After funds are released:** we still honour valid refunds and **recover them from the
  vendor's next payouts**.

## How you receive it

Refunds are returned to your **mobile money**, or to **store credit** when the wallet is
available. You will get a WhatsApp and SMS confirmation once a refund is processed.

To start a refund, open the order and report the issue, or read **Returns — faulty, wrong or
not as described**.

## One marketplace for Zambia

Vergeo5 brings **products, services and events** into a single, fast, mobile-first app.
Compare vendors selling the same product, request quotes for services, or buy tickets to
events — all priced in Kwacha and protected by escrow.

## Why people use Vergeo5

- **Escrow on every order** — your money is held until you confirm delivery.
- **Mobile money first** — pay with MTN or Airtel, or use a card.
- **Built for slow networks** — light pages that load fast on 3G.
- **WhatsApp updates** — order news reaches you where you already chat.

## At the entrance

- Open the ticket **in the app** so the QR is live — it **rotates every minute** for
  security.
- Let the organiser **scan the QR**.
- Provide your **PIN** if asked.

## Why screenshots do not work

Because the QR rotates, an old screenshot will not scan. This stops tickets being copied or
shared without permission. Keep your phone charged and open the app when you arrive.

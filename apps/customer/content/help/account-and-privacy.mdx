## Signing in

Sign in with your **phone number** and a one-time code (OTP), or with email or Google where
available. Keep your number up to date so you receive order updates.

## Your data

We handle your information in line with **Zambia's data-protection law**. You can view and
update your details in your account, and request help with your data at any time by
contacting support.

Sensitive documents such as ID photos are stored privately and used only for verification.

## When change-of-mind returns apply

Change-of-mind returns are **optional per listing**. Where a vendor allows them, the listing
shows a return window (usually **48 hours to 7 days**) and the item must be **unused and in
its original condition**.

## What you get back

Your refund is the **item price minus**:

- outbound delivery,
- return transport, and
- a **restocking fee** (typically 10%).

Refunds go to mobile money, or to store credit when the wallet is available. For faults or
wrong items instead, see **Returns — faulty, wrong or not as described**, which is always a
full refund.

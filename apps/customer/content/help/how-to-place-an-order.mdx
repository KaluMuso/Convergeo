## Placing your first order

1. **Find what you need.** Search or browse, then open a listing to see the vendor, price
   and delivery options.
2. **Add to cart.** You can buy from more than one vendor in a single visit.
3. **Choose delivery or pickup.** Delivery is available in Lusaka; pickup with QR and PIN is
   available nationwide.
4. **Pay.** Choose mobile money, card, or cash on delivery for eligible orders.
5. **Track it.** Follow your order status from your account and via WhatsApp updates.

Your payment is **held in escrow** until you confirm delivery — see **How escrow protects
your money on Vergeo5**.

## Where we deliver

- **Lusaka** — door delivery arranged by our team, with fees based on distance zones.
- **Free delivery** on Lusaka orders **over K200**.
- **Nationwide** — vendor listings are visible everywhere. Fulfilment is by **pickup**
  (QR and PIN) or **bus / courier freight** arranged case by case.

## How delivery is handled

For Lusaka, our operations team books a trusted local courier and shares tracking with you
over WhatsApp and SMS as your order moves.

Delivery fees are always shown at checkout before you pay.

## Getting your ticket

1. Open an event and choose a ticket type — **fixed price**, a **tier**, or a **free RSVP**.
2. Pay by mobile money or card (free RSVPs need no payment).
3. Your ticket appears in the app with a **secure QR code** and **PIN**.

## Keep it handy

Your ticket QR **rotates for security**, so open it from the app at the entrance rather than
relying on a screenshot. See **Using your ticket at the door**.

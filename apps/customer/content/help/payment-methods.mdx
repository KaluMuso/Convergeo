## Ways to pay

- **Mobile money** — MTN and Airtel push payments. Approve the prompt on your phone.
- **Card** — debit or credit card through a secure hosted checkout.
- **Cash on delivery** — available for orders **up to K500** in eligible areas.

Whichever method you choose, your money is **held in escrow** until delivery is confirmed.

## Prices are always in Kwacha

Every listing shows the price in ZMW, including any delivery fee before you pay.

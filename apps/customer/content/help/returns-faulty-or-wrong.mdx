## You are covered

If your item is **faulty, wrong, or not as described**, you are protected. This lane is
mandatory for every vendor and aligned with consumer-protection rules.

## How to report

1. Report the problem **within 48 hours** of delivery.
2. Add **photo evidence** of the issue.
3. We review and, where valid, issue a **full refund including delivery** from escrow.

Return shipping for faulty or wrong items is **charged to the vendor**, not to you. If a
vendor disputes the claim, Vergeo5 admin arbitrates fairly.

Because your money is held in escrow, a valid claim is refunded **without chasing the
vendor**.

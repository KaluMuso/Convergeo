## Your payment is protected

Every order on Vergeo5 is covered by escrow. When you pay, the money is **held by Vergeo5**
— not passed to the vendor — until your order is confirmed as delivered. If the order never
arrives or is not as described, you are protected.

## We never pool your money unsafely

Collected funds settle to a regulated, Lenco-managed account, and a platform ledger drives
every release and payout. Vergeo5 does not sit on your cash in a private account.

## What makes a payment safe

- Funds are **held in escrow** until delivery is confirmed.
- Refunds for faulty or undelivered orders come **straight from escrow**.
- You get status updates on WhatsApp and SMS at every step.

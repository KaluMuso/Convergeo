## Getting paid

When a buyer confirms delivery (or the automatic window passes), escrow **releases** and we
pay you out:

- **Mobile money** — usually within minutes, always within 48 hours.
- **Bank transfer** — typically 24–36 hours.

## Refunds and clawbacks

If a valid refund is needed after you have been paid, the amount is recovered from your
**next payouts**. Keeping accurate listings and fulfilling promptly avoids most disputes.

See **How escrow protects your money on Vergeo5** to understand the buyer's side of the flow.

## Release happens on delivery confirmation

The moment you confirm your order arrived, Vergeo5 releases the held funds and pays the
vendor. Confirming early is a kindness to small businesses — it speeds up their payout.

## Automatic fallbacks

If you forget to confirm, escrow still releases safely:

- **48 hours** after an order is marked delivered, it auto-confirms.
- **7 days** after shipping, as a final fallback if delivery was never recorded.

## Vendor payout timing

Once released, vendors are **paid out within 48 hours — usually in minutes on mobile
money**. Bank transfers can take 24–36 hours.

If you have a problem, report it **before** the confirmation window closes so funds stay in
escrow while we help.

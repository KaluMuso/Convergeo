## The short version

When you pay on Vergeo5, **your money does not go straight to the vendor.** It is held
safely in escrow — a protected holding account — until you confirm that your order
arrived. Only then do we release the funds to the vendor.

> You paid → Held by Vergeo5 → Released to the vendor

This is the single most important thing to understand about buying on Vergeo5: you are
never sending money and simply hoping a stranger delivers.

## Why escrow matters in Zambia

Buying online usually means trusting someone you have never met. Escrow removes that risk.
The vendor knows the money is real and waiting, so they are motivated to ship quickly. You
know the vendor cannot disappear with your cash, because Vergeo5 is holding it until you
are satisfied.

## How it works, step by step

1. **You pay.** Choose mobile money, card, or cash on delivery for smaller orders. Your
   payment is captured and moved into escrow, not to the vendor.
2. **We hold the funds.** Your order status shows **Held by Vergeo5**. The vendor can see
   that payment is secured and prepares your order.
3. **The vendor ships or prepares pickup.** You receive updates on WhatsApp and SMS as the
   status changes.
4. **You receive your order.** For delivery, you confirm receipt. For pickup, you show your
   QR code and PIN.
5. **Funds are released.** Once delivery is confirmed — or after the automatic confirmation
   window — we release the money to the vendor and pay them out.

## When are funds released?

Escrow releases on **delivery confirmation**. To keep things moving, there are automatic
fallbacks:

- **48 hours** after an order is marked delivered, it auto-confirms if you have not already.
- **7 days** after shipping is the final fallback if a delivered status was never recorded.

You can always confirm early once your order arrives — that releases the vendor's payout
faster and helps small businesses.

## What if something goes wrong?

This is exactly what escrow is for. If your item is **faulty, wrong, or not as described**,
report it within 48 hours with a photo. Because your money is still held by Vergeo5, we can
issue a **full refund from escrow** — you are not chasing the vendor for your money back.
See **Returns: faulty, wrong or not as described** for the full process.

If funds have already been released, refunds are still honoured — we recover them from the
vendor's next payouts.

## Does escrow cost me anything?

No. Escrow protection is built into every order at no extra charge to you. Vendors pay a
small commission on completed sales, shown to them before they publish a listing.

## Key points to remember

- Your money is **held by Vergeo5**, never sent straight to the vendor.
- Funds release **only after delivery is confirmed**, with 48-hour and 7-day safety nets.
- Problems reported in time are **refunded from escrow** — safely and quickly.
- Escrow is **free** for buyers.

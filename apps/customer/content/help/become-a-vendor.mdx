## Selling in a few steps

1. **Sign up as a vendor** and tell us about your business.
2. **Complete KYC** — a quick identity check so buyers can trust you.
3. **Create listings** for products, services, supplies or events.
4. **Fulfil orders** and get **paid out within 48 hours** — usually minutes on mobile money.

## Fair, transparent fees

Commissions vary by category and are **always shown before you publish** a listing. There is
no charge to list — you only pay commission on completed sales.

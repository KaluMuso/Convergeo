## Why we verify vendors

Verification keeps the marketplace trustworthy. Buyers know a real, identifiable business is
behind each listing.

## What you need

- A clear photo of your **NRC**.
- A **selfie** for a match check.
- A **mobile-money name that matches** your details, so payouts reach you.

Basic sellers can often **go live the same day**. New sellers start with sensible limits —
for example a cap on listings and on order values for the first few orders — which increase
as you build a track record.

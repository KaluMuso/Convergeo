## Where to see your order

Open **your account** to see every order and its current status — from _Held by Vergeo5_ to
_Delivered_. You will also receive updates on **WhatsApp** and **SMS** as the status changes.

## Common statuses

- **Held by Vergeo5** — payment secured in escrow, vendor preparing your order.
- **Shipped / Ready for pickup** — on the way or ready to collect.
- **Delivered** — received; confirm to release the vendor's payout.

Not seeing an update you expected? Message us on WhatsApp with your order number.

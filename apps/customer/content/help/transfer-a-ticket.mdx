## Sending a ticket to someone else

If you cannot attend, you can **transfer your ticket to a friend** from the app. Once
transferred:

- The ticket moves to their account with a fresh **QR and PIN**.
- Your copy is deactivated so only one valid ticket exists.

This is a simple friend-to-friend transfer. Reselling tickets on a marketplace is not
supported.

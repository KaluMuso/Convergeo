## Mobile money made simple

1. Choose **mobile money** at checkout and enter your number.
2. A **push prompt** appears on your phone — approve it with your PIN.
3. Your payment is confirmed and moved into **escrow**.

## If the prompt does not arrive

- Check you have network and enough balance.
- Confirm the number you entered is correct.
- Try again, or dial your provider's USSD code to approve a pending request.

Still stuck? Message us on WhatsApp and we will help you complete the payment.

## Safe, verified pickup

Pickup is available **nationwide**. When your order is ready, you receive a **QR code and a
PIN** in the app and by message.

1. Go to the pickup point when your order is marked ready.
2. Show your **QR code** to the vendor or agent.
3. Confirm with your **PIN**.
4. Handover is verified and your order is complete.

Because pickup is verified, both you and the vendor are protected — escrow releases only
once collection is confirmed.

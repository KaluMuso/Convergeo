## Cash on delivery (COD)

For eligible orders **up to K500**, you can pay cash when your order is delivered. This
keeps things simple for smaller purchases.

## Orders above K500

To keep everyone safe from fraud, orders **over K500** are not available for cash on
delivery. Instead you can:

- **Pay at pickup** by mobile money push, or
- **Prepay** by mobile money or card before dispatch.

Prepaid orders are still fully protected by escrow until you confirm delivery.

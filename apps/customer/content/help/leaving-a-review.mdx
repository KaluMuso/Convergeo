## Reviews you can trust

Only **verified buyers** can leave a review on Vergeo5. That means the star ratings and
comments you read come from people who **actually purchased** the product or service.

## How to review

1. Open a completed order in your account.
2. Give a **1–5 star** rating and write a short comment.
3. Add **photos** if they help other buyers.

Honest, specific reviews help good vendors stand out and help everyone shop with confidence.
